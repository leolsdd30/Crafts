# View: Craftsman Dashboard

## Idea & Main Objective
The `dashboard.php` inside `/views/craftsman` is the homepage for a worker after logging in.

## Purpose & Role
Shows them incoming "Requested" jobs and their immediate upcoming appointments.

## Required Code & Logic
- A feed of new `requests_bookings` where they are the target craftsman.
- "Accept & Quote" or "Decline" buttons.
- A widget showing their overall star rating and next 3 upcoming jobs.
