# View: Craftsman Earnings

## Idea & Main Objective
The `earnings.php` view gives the worker full financial transparency.

## Purpose & Role
Shows how much money they have made, what is currently stuck in Escrow, and allows them to download tax invoices.

## Required Code & Logic
- Displays data from the `Transaction` and `Invoice` models.
- "Total Earned", "Pending in Escrow", and "Total Platform Fees Paid" widgets.
- A list of downloadable PDF links generated by the Invoice system.
