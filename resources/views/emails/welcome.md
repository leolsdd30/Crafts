# Email: Welcome

## Idea & Main Objective
The `welcome.php` view is the first marketing email a new user receives.

## Purpose & Role
Sets the tone for the platform, validates their email address, and tells them what to do next.

## Required Code & Logic
- Clean, brand-aligned colors (inlined CSS).
- Dynamic logic: If the user is a Craftsman, the email body instructs them to complete their "ID Verification". If they are a Homeowner, it links them to the "Search" page.
